What is FeatherBB?

FeatherBB is an open source forum application released under the GNU General Public Licence.
It is free to download and use and will remain so.
FeatherBB is a fork of FluxBB 1.5 based on Slim Framework and designed to be simple and very lightweight
Maybe more? You are more than welcome to join the development :-)

Designed for developers

Our code has been built to be very easy to understand with a clear cut MVC architecture and an object-oriented architecture.

Time saver

Setup your forum within minutes thanks to Composer. Don't like it? No problem, you can download the archive and you're ready to go!

SEO-friendly

We have beautiful URLs and more options to get your website on the first page of your favorite search engine.

Mobile-friendly

Make sure your users won't miss a thing from your FeatherBB forum. They can access it from any terminal since our theme is reponsive.

Easy to customise

Install a plugin with a single click. Why should you bother to edit the core when it is so simple to customise your forum?

Use it in your native language

We use Gettext and po/mo files to provide you the localisation you need. If your language is not available, all you need to do is open Poedit!
